Privacy policy

At Yard Growers, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our marketplace application.

Information We Collect

User Registration: To provide you with a personalized experience, we collect information during the registration process, including your name, email address, and password.
Profile Information: Growers may voluntarily provide additional information such as location, product details, and farming practices to enhance their profiles.
Transaction Details: When making purchases, we collect necessary transaction information, including payment details, to facilitate secure and seamless transactions.
Data: We may collect information about your interactions with the application, such as browsing history, product views, and preferences.

How We Use Your Information

Personalization: We use collected data to personalize your experience, providing relevant product recommendations, and tailoring content based on your preferences.
Transaction Processing: Your information is essential for processing transactions, confirming orders, and ensuring the smooth operation of our marketplace.
Communication: We may use your contact details to communicate order updates, promotions, and relevant information. You can opt out of promotional communications at any time.

Data Security

Secure Transactions: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your payment information and ensure secure transactions.
Account Protection: Your account is safeguarded by a password. It's crucial to keep your password confidential and notify us if you suspect any unauthorized access.

Information Sharing

Grower-User Interaction: To facilitate the buying and selling process, certain information, such as delivery addresses and product details, may be shared between growers and buyers.
Third-Party Services: We may share data with trusted third-party services that assist us in operating the application, improving user experience, and conducting analytics.

Your Choices

Account Control: You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time.
Communication Preferences: You can manage your communication preferences, including opting out of promotional emails.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically for updates.
By using the Yard Growers Marketplace Application, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at [contact@yardgrowers.com].
